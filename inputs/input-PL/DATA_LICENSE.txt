DATASET ATTRIBUTION AND LICENSING

This software includes pre-processed snippets of the defined environmental rasters in the models dataset. 
These rasters are distributed under open licenses that allow redistribution with attribution.

1. LandScan HD (Oak Ridge National Laboratory)
   Being served under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license.
   This work utilizes the LandScan HD dataset (2022) provided by Oak Ridge National Laboratory.

2. Global Human Settlement Layer
   Primary source of the data is European Commission, Joint Research Centre shared under CC BY 4.0 license.
   Schiavina M., Melchiorri M., Pesaresi M. (2022): GHSL Data Package 2022.

3. Copernicus DEM
   Data provided thanks to European Space Agency and shared under Copernicus WorldDEM-30 license.
   © DLR e.V. 2010-2014 and © Airbus Defence and Space GmbH 2014-2018 provided under COPERNICUS by the European Union and ESA; all rights reserved.

4. Copernicus Land Monitoring Service
   Data by European Environment Agency with full, free and open access.
   Datasets used:
     - CORINE Land Cover 2018
     - High Resolution Layer: Tree Cover Density 2021
     - Water Bodies 2020 (300m)
   Data produced thanks to Copernicus data and information funded by the European Union - EU-Copernicus program.

5. ECOSTRESS Land Surface Temperature
   Provider of the data is NASA / JPL-Caltech. License is NASA Open Data Policy.
   Data provided by NASA/JPL ECOSTRESS mission.

Users of HRMTA must retain this attribution file when redistributing the 
environmental dataset.
